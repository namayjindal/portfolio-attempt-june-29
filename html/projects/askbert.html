Overview

In May 2021, I began Google's UX Design Course on Coursera to delve into a field that I had always been intrigued by, but never really understood. Over the course of 5 months, I learned about user research, inclusive and equitable design, wireframing, prototyping, and how to deliver incredible user experiences. An over-arching design project was assigned to us in the second month of the course. My prompt was - 'Design a mobile food-ordering app for a food truck based in London'.
